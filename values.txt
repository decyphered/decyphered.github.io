------------------

values
-------------------

public_education
  support the systems that keep learning accessible to everyone

privacy
  protect the data people trust us with

ethical_tech
  build useful technology with people, privacy, safety, and impact in mind

open_tools
  prefer tools that can be inspected, adapted, improved, and shared

curiosity
  make technology easier to question, understand, and use

knowledge
  expertise should open doors, not guard them

community
  build each other up; better systems come from shared effort

environment
  design systems with energy, hardware, and waste in mind
