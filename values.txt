------------------

values
-------------------

public_education
  support the systems that keep learning accessible to everyone

privacy
  protect the data people trust us with

open_tools
  prefer tools that can be inspected, adapted, improved, and shared

curiosity
  make technology approachable enough for people to ask better questions

ethical_tech
  use software, automation, and AI to serve people

environment
  remember that digital systems have physical costs

community
  build each other up; better systems come from shared effort

principle
  expertise should open doors, not guard them
