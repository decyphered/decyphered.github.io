------------------

values
-------------------

public_education
  serve the shared infrastructure of learning

privacy
  protect the data people trust us with

transparency
  make systems, decisions, and tradeoffs understandable

open_tools
  prefer software that can be inspected, adapted, and improved

curiosity
  make it safe to ask questions and learn out loud

ethical_tech
  use software, automation, and AI in ways that serve people

environment
  remember that digital systems have physical costs

community
  build each other up; we are all connected

principle
  expertise should open doors, not guard them
