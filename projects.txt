------------------

home-lab
-------------------

Single-machine linux home lab for learning open-source infrastructure,
containerized deployment, monitoring, local AI workflows, and web hosting.

Stack:
  Ubuntu Server LTS
  Docker
  Komodo
  Caddy
  Forgejo
  Uptime Kuma
  Prometheus
  Grafana
  PostgreSQL
  Ollama
  Open WebUI
  Langfuse

Principle:
  simple host, containerized services, Git-tracked config,
  internal-first access, documented.
